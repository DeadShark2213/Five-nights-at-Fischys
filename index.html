# Five Nights at Fischys ## Game Features - Office view with left/right mouse look. - Power management system. - Door controls. - Camera system with 8 cameras throughout the pizzeria showing different rooms. - Two player-chosen animatronics that move through rooms and jump scare the player. - 5 nights of increasing difficulty. - Time progression from 12 AM to 6 AM; every 50 seconds in real-time equals 1 hour in-game. - Night transitions with confetti and a black screen showing the night number. - Battery drains when doors are closed (10% per 15 seconds). - Animatronics visible only on cameras until they reach the doors. - Mouse movement to the left/right edge of the office view rotates the 3D-like perspective. - Mouse movement to the bottom of the screen reveals the camera button. - Full pizzeria theming with dark and scary visuals.
